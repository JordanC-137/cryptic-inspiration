70 famous historical quote examples for motivation

Updated 10 March 2023

The insights and experiences of successful historical leaders can be a valuable source of inspiration. Historical quotes can provide you with guidance and wisdom to succeed as a history student, teacher or history aficionado. Reviewing the words of famous leaders may help you maintain your motivation and make you more courageous. In this article, we list 70 famous examples of historical quotes to inspire you at work and at home.

70 examples of historical quotes

Some of the well-known historical quotes can be a source of valuable advice in nearly any situation. Consider these quotes for inspiration:
